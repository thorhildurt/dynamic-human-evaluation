Naturalness, sometimes referred to as fluency, is defined as whether you think a native speaker could have produced the utterance.

Example:

Sentence A: They have the best burgers in the valley

Sentence B: He not good

Selection: Sentence A

Explanation: Sentence A is something that a native speaker would say, but not sentence B since sentence B is an incomplete sentence.

If you are confident that both sentences are equally relevant in terms of naturalness (or equally irrelevant), select either one of the sentences.
